This course will offer a review of respiratory failure, oxygen delivery systems and interpretation of arterial blood gasses, all of which are crucial elements in understanding the human respiratory system and the appropriate resources for treating various respiratory conditions. 

The course consists of 6 chapters, with 9 articles and 23 questions in total. For the chapters that have questions, we will ask an array of questions to assess your skills and identify existing knowledge gaps before going into the theory.
